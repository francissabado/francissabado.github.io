Physical Design of Synchronous MSP4302015

NCL / MTNCL / Circuit Design

About Project

Lead Physical Design Engineer in the Synchronous openMSP430. Synthesized the MSP430 design using Design Compiler. Tested and verified the physical implementation in a custom low power Null Convention Logic library (Multi-threshold Null Convention Logic). Developed the Input/Output logic used in the Asynchronous and Synchronous Design, includes a scan chains. The physical design completed in both GlobalFoundries 65nm and MITLL 90nm SOI process.
